Set Your Profile

You can add contact information, your photo, and change your password on the Profile page. You can also resend the verification email, if you did not receive it.

When you first sign up, your initials appear in a circle on the top right. To set your profile:
  1. Select the circle, then select User settings from the menu.
  2. Enter your Contact Info:
    1. Your user name and email address already appear in Contact Info. Change your name or email address if needed.
    2. If you did not receive a verification email in your inbox, select Resend verification email.
    3. Add your job title and company name if you wish. This information is not mandatory.
    4. Select I don't want to receive email invitations to projects or studies. if this is the case.
  3. Select and upload a photo:
    1. If you want to add a profile photo, select Photo in the menu and then Upload Photo, and upload one from your computer.
    2. Drag the circle on the magnification bar to zoom and reposition your photo.
    3. When you are finished, be sure to select Crop and use. The photo is updated on the profile page.
  4. If you want to change your password, select Change Password and make the appropriate changes.
  5. Select Organization. The first time you log in to the application, you log in as a Tenant, which is the name of the organization in which you are currently working. You can have several tenants, and your current tenant may be different from your default organization. If you want to change your Default Organization, select it from the dropdown list. The change will be applied the next time you log in.
  6. When you are finished with your profile changes, select Save. The circle with your initials on the top right, now displays your photo.
