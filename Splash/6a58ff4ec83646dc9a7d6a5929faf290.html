Preview a Feedback Study

You can preview your feedback study to ensure it is ready before you publish it. SPLASH displays the study preview in a different tab to the tab it displays SPLASH application. The preivewed study is identical to the study displayed to participants and you can enter responses to the previewed study. Reponses to previewed studies are not saved.

Prerequisites

To preview a study the study must contain at least one image or prototype.

Procedure

On the Feedback tool page, click the Preview button.
The previewed study is displayed in a different tab.

Results

When you have examined the previewed study close the tab and return to SPLASH.
