Invite Team Members

You can invite your colleagues to join a project you created or projects you are a team member of.

Procedure

  1. On the SPLASH landing page, click the project thumbnail.

    The Project page is displayed.

  2. On the Project Team section on the Project page, select Invite Members.

    The Invite members dialog is displayed.

  3. Enter an email address and select Add.

    The email address is added to the List Of Inviteessection.

  4. You can invite more colleagues by entering an email address and selecting Add for each.
  5. Select the Send Invite button.

Results

The people you invite to join the project are sent an email inviting them to join the project. If they have told the SPLASH team they don't want to receive any email notifications, the invitation is displayed on their SPLASH Home page. For more information on accepting invitations to join a project, see Accept an Invitation.
