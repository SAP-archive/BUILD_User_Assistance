Settings

The project owner can pause or archive feedback studies. The project owner can restart paused or archived studies at anytime.

Note

When a study is created it is automatically active. Active studies are displayed on the Feedback results page and on the Project page.

Pausing Studies

Once you have paused a study, the Archive and Restart buttons become available in the feedback study on the Settings tab. It is not possible to retrieve the study-link for paused studies from the feedback study, and participants can't enter responses.
Note Paused studies are displayed from the Paused tab on the Feedback results page.

Archiving Studies

It is not possible to retrieve the study-link for archived studies from the feedback study, and participants cannot enter responses.
Note

Archived studies are displayed from the Archived tab on the Feedback results page.

Restarting Studies

Once a feedback study is restarted it is again possible to retrive the study-link for the study.
