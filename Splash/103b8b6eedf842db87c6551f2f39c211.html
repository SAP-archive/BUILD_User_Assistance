Create, Edit, and Delete Topics

Create a topic in any of the Community forums to start a discussion.

Context

You need to sign in or create an account before you can create or post responses to a topic.

Procedure

  1. In the Community page, select any of the following forums you want to create a topic in: Gallery, Tools, Learning, Design Services, Conference Highlights.
  2. Select the Create new topic button.

    The Create new topic dialog displays.

  3. Give your topic a title and description.
    1. In the Subject field, enter the title for your topic.
    2. In the Message field, enter the description for what you want to say.
  4. Select Create.

    Your topic is displayed.

  5. To edit your topic, hover your mouse over the text and a pencil icon appears. Select the text to modify your post.
  6. To delete your post, select the garbage can icon on the top right of your post.
