Restore Archived Projects

You can restore archived projects anytime.

Prerequisites

You must have archived at least one project.

Procedure

  1. On the Project page, select archived
    Your archived projects are displayed.
  2. Click Restore.
    The project is restored, and the project thumbnail is displayed on the Project page.

Results

The project is restored and is again available for all team members.
