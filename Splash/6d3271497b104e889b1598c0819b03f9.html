Preview Prototype

Preview your prototype pages and test out the interactions before you publish or share with others.

Prerequisites

Before you can preview your prototype pages, you must first add some pages and UI controls, if you want to test the interactions from one page to another.

Procedure

  1. In the UI Editor, open one of your pages.
  2. Select Preview on the toolbar to view your page.
  3. Select buttons, hotspots, and other controls you added to the pages and test the actions. Previewing your prototype displays the prototype pages and interactions as users will see them when using your application.
  4. Select the device at the top to view your pages in other formats like tablet, phone, or desktop.
  5. Select the Editor button on the toolbar to return to the UI Editor.
