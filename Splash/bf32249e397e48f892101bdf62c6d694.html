Participate in Feedback Studies

To particpate in a SPLASH feedback study, participants must receive by email a unique study-link from a team member of the project. The participants open the link in their browser, respond to questions about each image, and attempt to complete each task. If a participant has opted out of receiving email notifications from SPLASH, a study tile for the study is displayed in the Studies I Participate In section on your Projects page, and you select the study tile to participate.

Context

You can participate in SPLASH feedback studies using Google Chrome on Windows and Mac OS.

Procedure

  1. To open the study, open the email invitation, copy the unique BUILD user research study-link and open it in your Chrome browser. If you have opted out of receiving email notifications from SPLASH, click the study-tile in the My feedback section on your Project page.

    The page feedback study landing page is displayed, including the study name and description. The home page for the study displays the study name and description.

  2. If you select the I want to be kept anonymous check box your SPLASH account unsername is displayed with your responses.
  3. To start responding select Start Study.

    The first image or task is displayed.

  4. Answer questions as follows:
    • For questions that require you to answer through annotation only, respond by selecting on the image to drop Annotation icons , and optionally enter a comment for each icon. You can move previous annotation icons you have dropped, and edit the comments.
    • For questions that require you to enter a text answer, enter an answer in the text box provided. You can also select on the image to drop Annotation icons and add additional comments.
    • For multiple-choice questions, respond by selecting one or more of the multiple-choice answers provided. It may be possible to select more than one answer in reponse to the question. You can also select the image to drop Annotation icons and add additional comments.
  5. Display the next question or task by selecting Next when you have entered your answer to a question.

    If the next item in the study is a question, it is displayed. If the next item is a task, the Task dialog displays user guidance about how to complete the task.

  6. For tasks, you must interact with the prototype and attempt to complete the task that is displayed in the User Guidance text box in the right-hand penel .
  7. In tasks you can select the Abandon button in the right panel at any time to quit the task.
  8. In tasks, you can provide feedback at anytime by selecting Leave and View Feedback and dropping Annotation icons on the image, and selecting the icons to leave comments.
  9. When you have finished providing feedback, select Done.
  10. When you have completed the task select I Have Finished This Task.

    The result is displayed in the right panel.

  11. When you have responded to all the questions and tasks in the study, select Done.

Results

Participants' responses are now available to the study-creator and other members of the project from the Active tab on the Feedback results page and the Project page.
