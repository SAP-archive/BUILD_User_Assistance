What is SPLASH?

SPLASH is a UX service that provides design and user experience best practices, tools, and e-learning to organizations looking to implement a design-led and user-centered approach to innovation.

SPLASH is the go-to place for to drive innovation through a user-centered, design-led approach, which provides access to best practices content, prototyping and user research tools, design concept learning, and services to scale and accelerate the development process.

SPLASH features include:

  • LEARN Learn design concepts and best practices method cards..

  • DO Prototyping tool includes the UI Editor and Data Editor to create interactive prototypes without writing a single line of code. Research tool allows you to easily gather user feedback on prototypes to ensure you build the applications users want.

  • INSPIRE: Gallery includes sample apps you can use to create your own customized apps. You can also publish your own apps to the Gallery and share with others.
