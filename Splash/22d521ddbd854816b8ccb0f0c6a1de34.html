Create SPLASH User

To access more advanced features, you should sign up for a SPLASH account . You may have limited access to some features until your admin sets permissions for you.

  1. Select Join on the Sign In page.
  2. Enter a name that you want your team members to see. This is your screen name.
  3. Enter a valid email address to verify your email.
  4. Enter a password to sign in. You can change your password later. Your password must be 6 to 40 characters long and include any combination of 3 of the following:
    • Uppercase letters
    • Lowercase letters
    • Numbers
  5. Select Join. You can sign in and start using SPLASH.
  6. Check your email for a Welcome email. If you do not receive one, check your spam folder for an email from do.not.reply@sap.com. If you still don't receive the email, check under Contact Info on the Profile page after you sign in and select Resend verification email.
  7. Open the email and select the link to verify your email address. Your account is now activated.
  8. Sign in and start using SPLASH.
