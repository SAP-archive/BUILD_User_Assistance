Access Learning and Community Pages

The Learning and Community pages provide educational support, e-learning materials, and an interactive space where you can actively participate and interact with user experience design experts. SPLASH users with no background in user experience design can utilize these resources and apply their knowledge to their app development.

You can access the Learning and Community pages when you are signed in or not signed in. If you are a first-time user, you will arrive on the SPLASH landing page. If you have an account, you can sign in to the SPLASH home page.

When you are:

  • Not signed in:

    They can access the sections from the header. They can sign up by selecting the Sign Up button on the top right corner of the SPLASH landing page.

  • Signed in:

    They can access the sections from the footer. The header will disappear and reappear in the footer. They are now on the SPLASH home page.

To access the Learning page:

  • Not signed in: Select the Learning page from the header.

  • Signed in: Select the Learning page from the footer.

To access the Community page:

  • Not signed in: Select the Community page from the header.

  • Signed in: Select the Community page from the footer.
