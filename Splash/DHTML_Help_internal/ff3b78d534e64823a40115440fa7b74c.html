Set Your Profile

You can add contact information, your photo, and change your password on the Profile page. You can also resend the verification email, if you did not receive it.

When you first sign up to BUILD, your initials appear in a circle on the top right. To change your profile:
  1. Select the circle, then select Profile from the menu.
  2. Your user name and email already appear in Contact Info. Change your name or email if needed. To opt out of BUILD emails, select the Opt out check box.
  3. To add a profile photo, select Upload Photo, then upload one from your computer.
  4. Drag the circle on the magnification bar to zoom and reposition your photo.
  5. When you are finished, be sure to select Crop and use. The photo is updated on the profile page.
  6. To change your password, select Change Password and make the appropriate changes.
  7. When you are finished with your profile changes, select Save. The circle with your initials on the top right, should now display your photo.
