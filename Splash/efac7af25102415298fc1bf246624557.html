Search Gallery for Projects

You can search the Gallery for projects by applying tags that filter projects. By default, the projects displayed in the Gallery are sorted as most recent.

Procedure

  1. From the SPLASH landing page, select the Gallery tab.
  2. In the search bar, you can enter keywords to find the project you are looking for.

    These keywords filter projects displayed in the search results. Depending on what you enter, the search field may offer suggestions to complete your search.

    The keywords appear as tags.

  3. To view more results, scroll down and select View More.
