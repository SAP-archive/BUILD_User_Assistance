Community Page Overview

The Community page is an internal community where SPLASH users can view, search, and participate in topics related to SPLASH and user experience design. Each forum is divided into topics, where you can find and share information and resources on a specific subject.

Note

You need to sign in or create an account in order to add comments to the topics.

You can explore the following forums:

  • All:

    View all the Communityforums on one page.

  • Gallery:

    View and post responses related to topics in the Gallery.

  • Tools:

    View and post responses related to topics in Tools.

  • Learning:

    View and post responses related to topics in Learning.

  • Design Services:

    View and post responses to topics related to Design Services.

  • Conference Highlights:

    View and post responses to topics related to Conference Highlights.
