Enterprise Page Overview

SPLASH for your Enterprise is a personalized version of SPLASH which you can customize for your organization. You can manage your own private gallery, choose which learning content you want made available, set your own design guidelines, and decide on your own project management.

To access the Enterprise page:

  • On the SPLASH landing page, scroll to the bottom. Select Learn More.

Features in Enterprise:

  • Private Gallery:

    Maintain your own projects in the Private Gallery. You can see which projects have been viewed, liked, and cloned the most by other SPLASH users. You can read comments that other SPLASH users have left on your projects.

  • Learning Content:

    Customize which learning content you want to be made available in your organization.

  • Design Guidelines:

    Choose which design guidelines you want to standardize for your organization. You can create your own guidelines or adopt some of SAP's Fiori guidelines.

  • Project Management:

    Manage your teams and projects through the Customizable Project Management Process.

  • Brand:

    Utilize the white-label service to build your own brand in the organization.

If you want to learn more about the release for SPLASH for Enterprise, select the Contact Us button on the bottom of the page.
