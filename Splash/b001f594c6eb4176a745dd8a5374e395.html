Retrieve Study Links

You can retrieve the study-link for an active feedback study at any time after you have created a study.

Procedure

To retrieve a study-link for a feedback study, do one of the following:
  • After you publish the study, copy the link from the Feedback participant invitation page.
  • On the Home page, rollover an active feedback study thumbnail and select the Get Link icon.
  • Select the Active tab on the Feedback results page, rollover the feedback study thumbnail and select Get Link.
