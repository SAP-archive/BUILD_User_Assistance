Accept an Invitation

You are invited to join SPLASH projects by email or, if you have opted out of receiving email notifications from SPLASH, a link is displayed on your SPLASH Home page.

Prerequisites

You must be invited by another member of the project.

Procedure

  1. Select the link in the invitation email, or, if you have opted out of receiving email notifications from SPLASH, select the invitation link on your SPLASH Home page.
  2. Select Accept.
  3. If you have a SPLASH account with the email address that received the invitation, you automatically join the project. If you do not have a SPLASH account, the SPLASH landing page displays and you can create your SPLASH account.
  4. Enter your details, and when you log in to SPLASH the project thumbnail is displayed on your SPLASH Home page.

Results

The project is displayed on your SPLASH Home page. In addition, an avatar with your name is displayed in the Project Team section on the Project page, which all team members can see.
