Data Models and the Data Editor

In BUILD, you can create and modify data models to organize data elements in your projects.

The Data Editor displays objects graphically on the screen. Drag objects and their relations directly in the Data Editor. You can add one or several objects manually, from a business catalog, or from an Excel file.
