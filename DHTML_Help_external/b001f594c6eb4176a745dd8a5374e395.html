Retrieving Study Links

You can retrieve the study-link for an active user research study at any time after you have created a study.

Procedure

To retieve a study-link for a user research study do one of the following:
  • Copy the link from the Link to your study text box on the page displayed after you publish the study.
  • Click the Get Link icon on the user research study tile in the Study Activity section on the Project page.
  • Click the Active tab on the Research and Feedback page.
