Invite Team Members

You can invite your colleagues to become team members of a project you created or of a project of which you are a team member .

Context

Procedure

  1. On the Home page click the tile for the project that you want to invite colleagues to.

    The Project page is displayed.

  2. Click the Add People button.

    The ADD PEOPLE dialog is displayed

  3. Enter the email addresses of a colleague you want to invite to join the project team, and click ADD.

    The email address is added to the LIST OF INVITEES section.

  4. You can invite more colleagues by entering an email address and clicking ADD for each.
  5. Click the Send Invites button.

Results

Colleagues you invite to become team members are sent an email inviting them to join the project. For more information on accepting invitations to join project, see Accepting an Invitation.
