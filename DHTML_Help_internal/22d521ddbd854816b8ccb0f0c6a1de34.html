Sign Up

The first time you access BUILD you need to sign up. It's free and easy to do.

  1. Enter a name that you want your team members to see. This is your screen name.
  2. Enter a valid email address. BUILD needs to verify your email.
  3. Enter a password to access BUILD. You can change your password later.
  4. Click Sign Up. BUILD sends an email to your address.
  5. Open your email account and click the link contained in the do.not.reply email.

    Your account is activated and you return to the BUILD page.

  6. Sign in and start using BUILD. You are ready to create a new project.
