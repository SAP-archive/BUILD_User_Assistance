Modify a Relation

You can modify a relation from the Relations tab in the side pane to the right of the canvas.

Context

You can modify a relation once you have selected the object to which you want to make the change.

Procedure

  1. Under the Relations tab, click the relation that you want to modify.

  2. In the Info screen, make the required changes. You can make changes to the:
    • Name
    • Target object
    • Cardinality
    • Read/write status

      You can also view the name of the foreign key used in the model to manage the relationships between data of the two objects in the relation.
