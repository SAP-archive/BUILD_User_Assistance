Pausing Studies

Project owners can pause a study at anytime.

Procedure

  1. On the Home page, select the project tile that contains the user research study.
  2. Select the Research button on the navigation bar.
  3. Select the study tile on the Active tab on the Research page.
  4. Select the Settings tab.
  5. Select the I want to pause this study check box, and Selecting the Pause button on the results page of the research study.

Results

The study is paused. The Archive and Restart buttons become available from the Settings tab on the Research page. It is not possible to retrieve the study link for paused studies from the research study, and participants can't respond to the study. Paused studies are displayed from the Paused tab on the Research page.
