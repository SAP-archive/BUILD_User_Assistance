User Research Studies

The BUILD user research module lets you to quickly create and share user research studies to gather detailed feedback from colleagues or end-users about images, project pages, or interactive HTML prototype applications.

To start, you create a new study, give it a name, and then add images or HTML prototypes to it. You enter questions to be answered about each image, and create tasks for participants to complete on HTML prototypes. Then you publish the study to generate a study-link, which you can send to third parties by email.

When study participants click the study-link, the study opens in their browser. If they don't have a BUILD account, the BUILD Sign Up page is displayed. Once in the study, participants have the option to keep their responses anonymous. As participants enter their responses, the project team members can view the responses on the Research page almost immediately.

Note

User research studies are created within a project. Therefore you must create a project, or be a member of project , before you can create a user research study. For more information, see Create Projects

  1. Log on to project BUILD.
  2. Open a project of which you are a team member.
  3. Create and publish a user research study about that project.
  4. Email the study-link to third parties.
  5. The third parties open the user research study in their browser and respond to the study questions.
  6. You and other project team members can view the participants' responses on the Research page of the project.
