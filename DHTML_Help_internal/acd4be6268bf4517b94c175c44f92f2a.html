Archive Projects

The owner of a project can archive and unarchive a project on the Settings page.. It is not possible to modify archived projects.

To archive a project, you click the project tile, do the following:

  1. On the Home page, click the Settings button.
  2. Click the I want to archive this project checkbox, and clicking the Archive button.
To unarchive a project, the owner can click the I want to unarchive this project checkbox on the Settings page, and click the Unarchive button.
