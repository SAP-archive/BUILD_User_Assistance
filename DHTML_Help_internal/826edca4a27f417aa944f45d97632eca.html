Data Models and the Data Editor

In BUILD, you can create and modify data models in your projects, allowing you to manage meaningful business data.

The BUILD Data Editor lets you view objects in a graphical mode on a central screen, or canvas.

You manage objects as well as their relations directly in the graph by dragging them from one part of the canvas to another. You can add one or several objects manually from an business catalog, or from a spreadsheet such as an Excel file.

To access the Data Editor, click inside a project, then select Go to Data Model from the Projet page.

When you enter the Data Editor, you land on the canvas where you create or import your objects.

Use the buttons in the toolbar to modify the page:

  • Go to UI Composer (paintbrush icon)
  • Add object or objects
  • Zoom in or zoom out
