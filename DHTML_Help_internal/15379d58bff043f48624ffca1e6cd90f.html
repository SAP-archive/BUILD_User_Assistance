Delete a Relation

You can delete a relation directly in the canvas or in the right pane.

Deleting from the Canvas
  1. Place your cursor over the relation that you want to delete.
  2. Click the Delete icon that appears.
Deleting from the Right Pane
  1. In the list of relations, place your cursor over the relation that you want to delete.
  2. Click the Delete icon that appears.
