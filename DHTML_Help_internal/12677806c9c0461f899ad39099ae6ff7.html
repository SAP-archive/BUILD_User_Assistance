Publish the Prototype

After create your list report and object page, you can view your prototype in a browser and share it.

Context

From one of the pages in your Smart Template prototype:

Procedure

  1. Select the Publish icon.

  2. Select the Publish button. BUILD creates a link to your prototype.

  3. Copy the link and paste it in your browser. The prototype opens in the browser.

    You can test the data using the filter, and you can navigate from one page to another by selecting the data rows. You can also share the link with another BUILD user.
