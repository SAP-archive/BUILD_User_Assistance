Set Your Profile

You may want to add more information about yourself to your profile.

When you first sign up to BUILD, a short profile is created and the first initial of your screen name appears in a circle in the upper-right corner of the screen.
  1. Click the circle, then select Profile from the menu.
  2. If you want to change your screen name, select Contact Info and enter a name.
  3. Leave your current email address or enter a new one.
  4. If you want to use a photo, click Photo, then upload one from your computer.
  5. If you want to change your password, click Password and make the appropriate changes.
